Digital Action Prop
Turn your phone into a physical soundboard. Select your sounds, activate the sensors, and move your device to trigger the audio and visual feedback.

Up/Down: Triggers the primary "Cocking" sound.

Left/Right: Triggers the secondary "Shooting" sound along with a muzzle flash.
